## CTA Strategy

### Types of CTAs to Use
1. **Primary CTAs** (most important actions)
   - "Get Started"
   - "Request Quote"
   - "Schedule Consultation"

2. **Secondary CTAs** (supporting actions)
   - "View Demos"
   - "See Pricing"
   - "Learn More"

3. **Tertiary CTAs** (low commitment)
   - "Browse Services"
   - "View Portfolio"
   - "Read Case Studies"

### CTA Placement
- Above the fold (hero section)
- End of each major section
- Service cards
- Pricing tables
- Bottom of every page
- Sticky/floating CTA (optional)

### CTA Copy Best Practices
- Action-oriented
- Clear value proposition
- Creates urgency when appropriate
- Mobile-friendly (tap-able size)
