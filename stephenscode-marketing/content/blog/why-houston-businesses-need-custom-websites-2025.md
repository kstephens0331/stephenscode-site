## The Template Website Trap

If you're a Houston business owner, chances are you've been tempted by the promise of a $29/month website from Wix, Squarespace, or GoDaddy. It seems like a no-brainer—get online fast, save money, and avoid the "expensive" web developer.

But here's what nobody tells you: **template websites are costing you customers every single day.**

I've been building websites for Houston businesses for 14+ years, and I've seen the same story play out dozens of times. A contractor, restaurant owner, or professional service provider launches a template site, gets frustrated with limitations, and eventually calls me to rebuild it properly. By then, they've lost 6-12 months of potential customers and thousands in revenue.

Let me explain why custom websites aren't just better—they're essential for growth in 2025.

## Template Websites: The Hidden Costs

### 1. Everyone Looks the Same

When you use a template, you're using the same design as thousands of other businesses. Your potential customers can't tell you apart from your competitors. In a market as competitive as Houston, standing out is everything.

**The result?** Customers pick based on price alone because they can't see what makes you different.

### 2. SEO Limitations

Google favors websites that load fast, provide unique content, and offer great user experiences. Template sites are bloated with features you don't need, load slowly, and often use the same content structure as everyone else.

**The result?** Your competitors with custom sites rank higher, and you're invisible in search results.

### 3. Can't Grow With You

Your business evolves. You add services, hire staff, open new locations. Template sites can't adapt. You're stuck with whatever features the platform offers, and custom functionality costs extra (or isn't possible at all).

**The result?** Your website holds your business back instead of propelling it forward.

### 4. You Don't Own Anything

With template platforms, you're renting. Stop paying, and your site disappears. Want to move to another platform? Good luck exporting your data and design. You're locked in.

**The result?** You've built your business on borrowed land.

## What Makes Custom Websites Better?

### 1. Built for YOUR Business

Every Houston business is unique. A custom website is designed around your specific services, target customers, and competitive advantages. No unnecessary features, no compromises.

**Example:** A plumbing company doesn't need a blog post feature—they need a booking system and emergency call buttons prominently displayed. A law firm needs case study showcases and client testimonials, not e-commerce features.

### 2. SEO That Actually Works

Custom websites are built with SEO baked in from day one. Clean code, fast loading times, unique content, and proper schema markup. I've helped clients go from page 5 to page 1 in Google just by rebuilding their site properly.

**Real result:** One Conroe contractor went from 2-3 leads per month to 15-20 per month after switching from Wix to a custom site.

### 3. Scales With Your Business

Need to add online booking? Customer portals? Inventory management? Multiple locations? Custom sites grow with you. We build the foundation right, so adding features later is straightforward.

**The difference:** Template sites make you adapt to their limitations. Custom sites adapt to your needs.

### 4. You Own Everything

With a custom website, you own the code, the design, the domain, the hosting—everything. Want to move hosts? No problem. Want another developer to maintain it? They can. You're in control.
