## How to Generate/Source:

### Option 1: AI Generation (Recommended)
Use Midjourney, DALL-E, or similar with prompts like:
- "Professional barbershop interior, vintage leather chairs, warm lighting, upscale, cinematic"
- "Professional barber cutting client hair, close-up, detailed, photorealistic"
- "Modern fade haircut result, professional photography, clean background"

### Option 2: Stock Photos
- Pexels.com (free, high-quality)
- Unsplash.com (free, professional)
- Shutterstock (paid, premium quality)

Search terms: "barbershop", "barber cutting hair", "men's haircut", "straight razor shave", "barbershop interior"

### Option 3: Real Photos
- Take photos at a real barbershop (with permission)
- Hire a photographer for a barbershop photoshoot
- Commission custom photography
